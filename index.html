Healthcare Under Attack — Infrastructure Analysis

verified

Gaza's healthcare system has been systematically dismantled. EXHIBIT F

MoH vs. Lancet — 34.7% Higher

estimated

The 60,199 identified victims documented by the Gaza Ministry of Health represents a verified, name-by-name count. However, a The Lancet study published in January 2025 estimated the true number of excess violent deaths at 75,200+ — a figure 34.7% higher. The discrepancy is largely driven by an estimated 10,000+ people still buried under rubble who have not been recovered or identified.

Methodological note: The Lancet estimate uses statistical modeling based on multiple data sources including hospital reports, burial records, media reports, and morgue counts. The 10,000+ missing under rubble represent victims who died but whose bodies could not be recovered due to ongoing hostilities, lack of heavy equipment, and the destruction of civil defense infrastructure. The true toll likely falls somewhere between these two figures.

EXHIBIT G

Data Sources & Methodology

archive

Data Collection: The Gaza Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Palestinian Ministry of Health in Ramallah, maintains a detailed registry of all identified victims. Each entry includes name, age, sex, and ID number, cross-referenced with hospital admission records, burial permits, and family testimonies.

Confidence Levels:

  • Verified — Confirmed by multiple authoritative sources including UN, WHO, and independent researchers
  • Reported — Reported by reliable sources but requiring additional cross-verification
  • Estimated — Statistical estimates based on available data and modeling
