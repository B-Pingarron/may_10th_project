In Memory of Mothers and Children

This Mother's Day, we honor the mothers and children whose lives have been torn apart by conflicts around the world. Behind each statistic is a human story — a mother who will never hold her child again, a child who will never know a mother's embrace. Their memory demands that we bear witness.

Healthcare Under Attack

Gaza's healthcare system has been systematically dismantled. Of 36 hospitals operating before the conflict, the vast majority have been destroyed or severely damaged, crippling the ability to care for the wounded and sick.
