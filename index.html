EXHIBIT B

Children & Women — 37,666 Lost

verified

Children and women constitute 37,666 identified victims (62.6%) — the overwhelming majority of those killed. Of the 18,457 children, 10,656 (57.7%) are boys and 7,801 (42.3%) are girls. Among 19,209 women and girls, 7,801 (40.6%) were under 18, 9,735 (50.7%) of reproductive age, and 1,673 (8.7%) were elderly.

EXHIBIT D

Healthcare Destruction & Undercount Analysis

verified

Gaza's healthcare system has been systematically dismantled. Of 36 hospitals operating before the conflict, only 10 (28%) remain functional. Meanwhile, the verified MoH count of 60,199 is estimated to be 34.7% lower than the true toll — The Lancet estimates 75,200+ excess violent deaths, with 10,000+ still buried under rubble.

Methodological note: The Lancet estimate uses statistical modeling based on multiple data sources including hospital reports, burial records, media reports, and morgue counts. The 10,000+ missing under rubble represent victims who died but whose bodies could not be recovered due to ongoing hostilities, lack of heavy equipment, and the destruction of civil defense infrastructure.

EXHIBIT E

Data Sources & Methodology

archive

Data Collection: The Gaza Ministry of Health, in coordination with the Palestinian Ministry of Health in Ramallah, maintains a detailed registry of all identified victims. Each entry includes name, age, sex, and ID number, cross-referenced with hospital admission records, burial permits, and family testimonies.

Confidence Levels:

  • Verified — Confirmed by multiple authoritative sources including UN, WHO, and independent researchers
  • Reported — Reported by reliable sources but requiring additional cross-verification
  • Estimated — Statistical estimates based on available data and modeling
