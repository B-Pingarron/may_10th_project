Conflict-by-Conflict Analysis

Gaza/Palestine (2023-2026)

  • Total killed: 42,000+ Palestinians
  • Injured: 96,000+
  • Children killed: 13,000+ (700+ babies)
  • Missing under rubble: 10,000+
  • Displaced: 1.9 million (90% of population)

Lebanon (2024-2026)

  • Total killed: 2,679+ since March 2, 2026
  • Injured: 8,200+
  • Children killed: 177
  • Women killed: 270
  • Medics killed: 131
  • Displaced: 1.2 million

Sudan (2023-2026)

  • Total killed: 40,000-59,000+ (true toll may exceed 150,000)
  • Children killed/maimed: 4,300+
  • Displaced: 14 million (world's largest displacement crisis)
  • Need humanitarian assistance: 34 million

Ukraine-Russia War (2022-2026)

  • Civilians killed: 15,578+
  • Injured: 41,378+
  • March 2026 casualties: 211 killed, 1,206 injured (49% increase)
  • Need humanitarian assistance: 10.8 million

Methodology

Data Collection: Multi-source triangulation from UN agencies, government sources, academic research, and NGOs.

Confidence Levels:

  • Verified - Confirmed by multiple authoritative sources
  • Reported - Reported by reliable sources but not independently verified
  • Estimated - Estimated figures based on available data
